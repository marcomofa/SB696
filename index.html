SB 969

Expanding Access Through Choice

What is SB 969?

SB 969 is a proposed policy that may expand how certain services are delivered.

  • Some services could be offered remotely
  • Remote services may become a permanent option
  • The focus is on individual choice and needs

Why It Matters

Access to services can look different for each person, family, and community.

  • Individuals may have more service options
  • Families can better understand available choices
  • Providers can support informed decision-making

Ways to Learn More

  • Ask questions about service options
  • Review how policy decisions may impact services
  • Find your state representatives if you want more information

About Options For All

Options For All creates and supports opportunities for individuals with disabilities to make choices, build independence, and live, work, and belong in their communities with dignity and respect.
